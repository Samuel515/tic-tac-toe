1. How to play
  2. Players: Two players, one using X and the other using O.
  3. Turns: Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell.
  4. Winning Condition: A player wins by being the first to place three of their marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. Draw Condition: If all cells are filled and neither player has three marks in a row, the game is a draw.
  6. Optimal Play: The game can end in a win for one player or a draw if both players play optimally.
